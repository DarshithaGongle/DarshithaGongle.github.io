How I got here

My Journey

2015 The spark Hyderabad, India

Growing up in the 2000s, the internet felt like magic. I spent hours on Google just trying to understand how webpages worked — what was behind the screen, who built them, how. Between sophomore and junior year of high school, I wrote my first lines of code. Back then, doing anything on a computer felt powerful. I held onto that feeling.

HTML/CSS Curiosity First code
2021 – 2025 The shift VJIT · Hyderabad

Somewhere in the middle of building Neutral Note, I stopped caring about the model accuracy and started caring about whether anyone would actually trust it. I was thinking about who would use it, why they'd be skeptical, and what we'd have to prove before they believed it. I hadn't written a single line of PM theory. I was just thinking like one.

Neutral Note SignVoice Product thinking B.Tech CS
Feb – Jul 2024 First real job RecVue · Hyderabad

RecVue was fast and unforgiving, exactly what I needed. In a matter of weeks I went from observing to owning: writing PRDs, running sprint planning, managing roadmaps, navigating stakeholder tradeoffs. I'd never done any of it formally before. I figured it out by doing it, often in the same meeting where I first encountered it.

PRDs Roadmap Agile Stakeholders B2B SaaS
2025 – Present Going deeper ASU · EdPlus · Tempe

At EdPlus I discovered something I hadn't expected — a genuine passion for accessibility. Small changes in a UI can make a product usable or unusable for someone with a physical or cognitive disability. That's not a trivial detail, that's the whole product. Alongside this, I'm completing my MS in CS at ASU with a specialization in AI/ML — going deeper on the technical side while staying grounded in user impact.
